How Anubhuti Works

Simple steps to share your internship story with our community

📝 Submit Your Story

Fill out a comprehensive form with details about your internship, role, organization, duration, and key learnings. Share your authentic experience with our community.

✅ Admin Review

Our dedicated team reviews each submission to ensure quality and relevance. We provide constructive feedback to enhance your story before publication.

📚 Published in Archives

Once approved, your experience is published in our archive volumes and becomes a valuable resource for future students seeking guidance and inspiration.

🌟 Inspire Others

Your story helps junior students understand real-world opportunities, industry insights, and career pathways. Your experience becomes their inspiration.

Before You Submit

A few quick checks that keep the submission process smooth

Keep your details accurate

Use your official name, valid email address, and correct roll number so your submission can be verified easily.

Write a clear summary

Include the role, responsibilities, learning outcomes, and any practical skills you gained during the experience.

Submit once

Each student can submit only once per form, so review your details before sending the form.

Check deadlines

Make sure the active form is still open before submitting your experience.
