Our Vision

Internship Programme is one of the unique learning experiences for a student which is meticulously designed and executed for at least 1 months for each student. It is one of the distinguishing features of DSVV education. This unique program not only guarantees students application of what they’ve learned in class, but also presents an opportunity for them to gain valuable career related experiences, make contacts in their chosen field, and better the world through their knowledge. It includes experiences in the field of health, education, alternative therapies, social service, mass awakening. Village adoption and model village Development Programme for ushering in a new social order based upon the vision of Gurudev is run by the students of the Department of Rural Management. Regular social services are given by the unit of National Social Service of university.
