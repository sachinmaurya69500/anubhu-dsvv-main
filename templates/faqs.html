Common Questions

Short answers to the things students usually ask first

Who can submit an experience?

Any registered student with an active form available on the Forms page can submit a story.

Can I submit more than once?

No. Each student can submit only once per form.

What happens after I submit?

The submission is reviewed by the team and then approved stories are published in the archive.

Why do I need to log in?

Logging in lets the site personalize your access and prevents duplicate submissions.
